XBOX RANKED: Unordered list

Apex Legends Ranked system is base on RP points that you get by killing, getting asssists from team kill and winning a game of ranked. The final goal is to reach the Apex Predator rank, which only the top 500 players on each platform (PC, PS4 and XBOX) can achieve

  • Predator: Top 500 players
  • Master: 10000+RP
  • Diamond: 7200-10000RP
  • Platinum: 4800-7200RP
  • Gold: 2800-4800RP
  • Silver: 1200-2800RP
  • Bronze: 0-1200RP

After the season ends you will earn a reward based on the rank achieved.
